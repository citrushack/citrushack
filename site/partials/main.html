FAQ

read this...
What is a Hackathon?
A hackathon is a competition in which students team up to create projects. Hackathons have nothing to do with illegal security breaches. See this for a good explanation.
What kind of project can I make?
Mobile apps, websites, games, and hardware mashups are popular choices. We will be providing a list of possible project ideas as well which we would like to see.
Am I eligible to come?
In order to attend you must be an undergraduate (or recent grad) student at an accredited school, and you must be at least 18 years of age. Unfortunately, we do NOT accept high school students at this time.
I don't have a team
You don't need to have a team per-se (although we encourage it). We will be having a small social before hacking starts so you can join/form a team if you don't already have one before coming.
Travel reimbursements?
We do not have any plans at this moment for reimbursing any kind of travel. We will be however, providing transportation for a select number of surrounding schools.
Why do I have to fill out an application?
We anticipate having a large number of students wanting to come to our event, and unfortunately we have a limit as to how many people we can physically fit in our venue. We will ensure there will be a good mix of beginner and veteran participants.
When will I know if I was accepted?
We will be sending out our first wave of acceptance emails around mid March. We will keep you informed.
Anything else?
As an official Major League Hacking sanctioned event, you (as well as we) MUST follow the code of conduct found here. Take some time to read through it.
